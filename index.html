Welcome to the Klingon Translations Project

This project is a free, open-source initiative to translate significant cultural, spiritual, and historical texts into the Klingon language. Our first major work is the translation of the Bible.

This project is maintained by Rees Farrington, with assistance from AI partners Jules and ChatGPT.

Explore the translations, study the Klingon language, and reflect on familiar texts in a new way.
