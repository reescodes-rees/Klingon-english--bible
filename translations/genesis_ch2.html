Genesis Chapter 2

Klingon–English Parallel Translation

Section 1: Completion of Creation (2:1–3)

English: The heavens and earth were completed, and God rested on the seventh day, making it holy.

Klingon: rIn chal tera’ je, ‘ej jaj SochDaq leS Qun, vaj lajqu’ ‘oH.

Section 2: The Garden of Eden (2:4–17)

English: The Lord God formed man from dust, placed him in the Garden of Eden to work and care for it, and gave him one command: not to eat from the tree of the knowledge of good and evil.

Klingon: joH Qun loD chenmoH, ghunDaq lan, vum ‘ej Qorgh. wa’ ra’ nob: Sor Sov QaQ mIw SopQo’.

Section 3: Creation of Woman (2:18–25)

English: The Lord God created a suitable helper for the man, forming a woman from one of his ribs. They were united and felt no shame.

Klingon: joH Qun be’ chenmoH loDvaD. porgh wa’ moj ‘ej lajqu’—pagh nuv nIDqu’.
